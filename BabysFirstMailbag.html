Baby's First Mailbag

An experimental session with a controversial product


NOTE: It is the dream, and maybe the entire motivation for this website, to publish a recurring mailbag where curious Kings fans come to me with questions that I answer.

It is the reality, however, that attention for this humble plucky site has been hard to come by. To receive readers' questions one must first have readers, and I don't.

Still, it stands to reason that if this is the dream, not only would I be well-served to train for it in the meantime, but doing so may even -- in a fit of reverse-engineering -- help to make the dream into reality. If I assure prospective readers of my Kings sense and knowledge ahead of time, they'll feel more inspired to write me queries, knowing they'll get a trusted response.

So, I recently logged onto OpenAI's ChatGPT and attempted to conduct a Q&A, soliciting the machine for questions, which I then answered. What follows is the complete and full text of that exchange...

***
